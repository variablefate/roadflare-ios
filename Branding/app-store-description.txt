Tired of faceless rideshare apps? RoadFlare lets you request rides from people you already know and trust for the job. Build your personal driver network, pay however you want, and it goes straight to your driver. No surge pricing. No platform fees. No middleman.

Privacy Nutrition Labels
------------------------
Data Not Collected.

RoadFlare does not collect user data. Location is encrypted end-to-end to the
driver via Nostr — never seen by RoadFlare. Display names are optional and not
linked to real identity. Nostr keypairs are generated and stored locally on
device. Nostr relays are third-party infrastructure; RoadFlare does not operate
or read data from them.
